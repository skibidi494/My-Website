Our goal for the Dachshund dog breed is to make sure that they are in the right home for them, and making sure they are being treated right. Before letting people adopt our Dachshunds, we always do a house inspection and have an interview with the owners to make sure our puppies are going to good homes. We hope that if dogs, not just Dachshunds, go to good homes, they will be treated better, making their behaviors more lively and happy! Dogs for LIFE!
