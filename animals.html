These animals are sometimes called hot dogs or wiener dogs because of their looks, but their breed name is Dachshund. Dachshunds have a long torso, making them look like hot dogs. They also have short and stubby legs, and when they run, they look so cute! Dachshunds do bark a lot, but that is what makes them so lively and lovable! Happy dogs make us happy!
